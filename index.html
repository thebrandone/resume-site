About Me

I am a life-long learner that has always been interested in computer programming. From a young age I knew I wanted to work with computers as I would always be the one in the family to be called on to fix the household computer. In High School I took many AP classes, AP Computer Science being one of them, and during that time I also worked as an Apprentice for a computational science non-profit. Programming is never a chore for me, it has always been something that I long to do. After completing the Bootcamp with UNC-Chapel Hill/Trilogy, I hope to gain employment in the field and further my skills.
